Catalogues

Overview


Catalogues define which Materials are visible and accessible to specific Users in SM2ARTA.


They help control access, simplify planning, and reduce the risk of selecting or ordering incorrect Materials.


A Catalogue is essentially a group of Materials assigned to one or more users based on their Project, Event, or functional responsibilities.

Key Points


  • Users can be assigned one or multiple Catalogues depending on their scope of work;
  • A user can only view and plan Materials within their assigned Catalogue(s);

  • The level of Catalogue segmentation can vary depending on project needs; Catalogues per Project and Event are recommended for greater control and easy setup.
