View Materials


1. Go to Materials - Overview in the Navigation Panel.



2. You will see the full list of Materials created in the system. 



3. To view more details about Materials, click the individual Expand icons before the SKU Code, or use Expand All to open all records. 


Use the Collapse icon or Collapse All to hide the additional details.



4. To adjust Measurement Unit Settings, use the dropdown menu located near the Action Bar. The interface will automatically switch to display values in the selected measurement system (Metric or US Units).



5.To add a single Material to multiple Catalogues, click View in the Contained in Catalogues field. In the drawer that opens, use the checkboxes to add or remove the Material from Catalogues as needed.
