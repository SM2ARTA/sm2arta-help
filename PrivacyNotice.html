Privacy Notice


Effective date: October 11, 2025


This Privacy Notice (“Notice”) explains how SM2ARTA LLC (“SM2ARTA,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the SM2ARTA platform and related services.


By using SM2ARTA, you agree to this Notice.


1. Who We Are


SM2ARTA is a cloud-based platform for materials and asset management, operated by SM2ARTA LLC, a company registered in the State of Florida, USA.


Hosting infrastructure is located in OVH data centers in Frankfurt, Germany (EU).


Contact: info@smmarta.com.


2. Information We Collect


We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve the Service, including:

a. Account Information


  • Name, email address, organization, and role when you register or are invited to use the platform.
b. Usage Data


  • Log files, IP addresses, device identifiers, browser type, session duration, and activity within the system.
c. Support & Communication


  • Information you provide when contacting support or sending feedback.
d. System & Operational Data


  • Configuration data, material records, and metadata uploaded or processed through SM2ARTA.


We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (e.g., financial, biometric, or health information).


3. How We Use Information


We use collected data to:


  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service;
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts;
  • Respond to inquiries and technical issues;
  • Monitor system security and performance;
  • Generate anonymized usage analytics;
  • Comply with legal and contractual obligations.


We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data for marketing purposes.


4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)


If you are located in the European Union or UK, processing is based on:


  • Contractual necessity — to provide the Service to you;
  • Legitimate interests — system operation, improvement, and security;
  • Legal compliance — fulfilling legal obligations;
  • Consent — where required for optional features (e.g., analytics).


5. Data Location & Security


  • Data is hosted on OVH Frankfurt (EU) servers.
  • All data in transit uses TLS encryption, and personal data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) where applicable.
  • Administrative access is limited via role-based access control (RBAC).
  • Logs are maintained for auditing and security monitoring.


While SM2ARTA takes reasonable precautions, no system can be completely secure, and you use the Service at your own risk.


6. Sharing & Disclosure


We may share limited data only with:


  • Service providers (e.g., SendGrid for email delivery, hosting partners);
  • Legal authorities when required by law, subpoena, or court order;
  • Successor entities in case of merger, acquisition, or restructuring (subject to equivalent protection).


All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations.


7. Data Retention


Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Service.


Backup data may persist temporarily for recovery but is automatically purged after a reasonable period.


Upon account closure or contract termination, data may be permanently deleted and unrecoverable.


8. Your Rights


Depending on your location, you may have rights to:


  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data;
  • Request export (portability) of your data;
  • Object to or restrict processing;
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.


Requests can be sent to info@smmarta.com. Verification may be required before we respond.


9. Cookies & Tracking


SM2ARTA uses only essential cookies for authentication and security.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Optional analytics, if enabled, are anonymized and used solely for performance monitoring.


10. International Transfers


Data may be accessed by SM2ARTA LLC (U.S.) under safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent mechanisms under GDPR.


By using SM2ARTA, you consent to such transfers where permitted by law.


11. Children’s Data


The Service is intended for professional use and not for children under 16.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.


12. Updates to This Notice


We may update this Notice from time to time. The revised version will be posted with a new effective date.


Continued use of SM2ARTA after an update means you accept the revised Notice.
