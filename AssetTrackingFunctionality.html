Asset Tracking Functionality


The Asset Tracking functionality in SM2ARTA provides the system mechanisms that enable the controlled execution of Asset Tracking SOPs across warehouses, distribution networks, and venues.


Asset Tracking in SM2ARTA is based on a simple and enforceable functional model:


  • Nomenclatures represent the physical assets being tracked;
  • Transactions record every operational activity affecting those assets;
  • Reports provide visibility, control, and audit evidence across the entire supply chain.


Together, these components ensure that all assets can be:


  • Tracked from procurement through final dissolution;
  • Accounted for at any location and at any point in time;
  • Reconciled against physical inventories and warehouse systems;
  • Audited with full documentary and photographic evidence.


SM2ARTA enforces the principle that no physical movement, adjustment, or exception is valid unless it is recorded as a system transaction.


This approach eliminates informal tracking, reduces operational risk, and ensures a single source of truth for event-level asset management.
