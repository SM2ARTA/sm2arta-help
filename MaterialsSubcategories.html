Materials Subcategories

Overview


Subcategories form the second level of the Materials hierarchy in SM2ARTA.


They organize Materials within a broader Category, providing additional detail for reporting, filtering, and operational management.


Examples


  • Category: Furniture
    • Subcategory: Chairs, Tables


  • Category: IT Equipment
    • Subcategory: Laptops, Printers, Network Devices


  • Category: Services 
    • Subcategory: Cleaning, Catering, Security


Key Points


Each Subcategory can contain one or more Materials, allowing admins to group related items — whether FF&E, IT, Overlay, Signage, or Services — in a clear and consistent way.


Subcategories can be created, renamed, or deleted within their parent category.


When naming subcategories:

  • Keep titles short and standardized
  • Use consistent terminology across venues and catalogues
  • Periodically review and remove duplicates or outdated entries
