Components Inventory


The Components Inventory serves as the record of all systems and services that make up the SM2ARTA™ platform. It outlines each component’s purpose, environment, ownership, and dependencies to support operational clarity and governance.


Key objectives:


  • Maintain a complete and accurate register of all platform components;
  • Clearly define ownership and accountability for each system or service;
  • Enable efficient troubleshooting, security assessments, and change management;
  • Provide traceability and oversight to support compliance requirements (e.g., SOC 2, ISO 27001).
