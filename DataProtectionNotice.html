Data Protection Notice


Effective date: October 11, 2025


This Data Protection & Security Notice (“Notice”) describes how SM2ARTA LLC (“SM2ARTA,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) protects data processed through the SM2ARTA platform.


This Notice supplements the Privacy Notice and the Terms of Service.


1. Our Commitment


SM2ARTA is designed with a strong focus on data protection, system integrity, and privacy. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard customer data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.


2. Hosting & Infrastructure


The SM2ARTA platform is hosted on OVH data centers located in Frankfurt, Germany (EU).


Infrastructure is maintained under redundant configurations, firewall protection, and regular monitoring.


Servers run on Linux environments (Ubuntu) with isolated Docker containers and enforced least-privilege access controls.


All administrative access is secured through multi-factor authentication (MFA) and role-based permissions.


3. Data Security Measures


  • Encryption in Transit:        All web traffic uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) connections.
  • Encryption at Rest:                Sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256 where applicable.
  • Access Control:                Role-based access control (RBAC) with audit logging; no shared credentials.
  • Network Security:                Firewalls, network isolation, and continuous intrusion detection via OVH tools.
  • Monitoring & Logging:        Application and system logs are retained for limited periods for auditing and security.
  • Backups:                         Encrypted automated backups stored separately outside OVH infrastructure.
  • Vulnerability Management:        Regular security updates and dependency patching within container environments.


4. Data Handling & Retention


Data is retained only for the duration necessary to deliver contracted services.


Backup copies are temporary and automatically purged within defined retention cycles.


Upon termination of service, customer data and associated backups may be permanently deleted and unrecoverable.


SM2ARTA does not guarantee long-term storage or data restoration beyond standard operational recovery procedures.


5. Incident Response


In case of a confirmed data-security incident:


  • SM2ARTA will investigate immediately to contain and assess impact.
  • Affected customers will be notified without undue delay after confirmation of material impact.
  • Incident details will include the nature of the event, affected data categories (if known), and mitigation actions taken.
  • Notification does not imply admission of fault or liability.


6. Customer Responsibilities


Customers are responsible for:


  • Managing user accounts, passwords, and access permissions;
  • Keeping contact information up to date for notifications;
  • Maintaining independent backups for critical information where required;
  • Ensuring compliance with local data-protection laws applicable to their own operations.


7. Compliance Framework


SM2ARTA aligns its security controls with widely recognized principles inspired by:


  • SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria (security, availability, and confidentiality);
  • ISO/IEC 27001 best-practice guidelines for information-security management.


SM2ARTA is not formally certified under these frameworks at this time but follows their core controls and continuous-improvement practices.


8. Sub-Processors & Third-Party Services


SM2ARTA engages limited, vetted providers to support its operations, including:


  • OVH (Frankfurt, EU) — hosting and infrastructure services;
  • SendGrid (USA) — transactional email delivery.


All sub-processors operate under contractual data-protection and confidentiality obligations.


9. Updates


This Notice may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected with a new effective date.


Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Notice.


10. No Warranties & Limitation of Liability

While reasonable measures are applied, no technology or process can eliminate all security risks.

Use of the SM2ARTA platform implies acceptance of these inherent risks and limitations.
