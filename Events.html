Events 

Overview


The Events interface is used to define and manage tournaments, test events, or other operational activities within SM2ARTA.


Once events are created, they will appear in the Material Planning module for further configuration and planning.


Each event can be configured by linking to it Venues, Spaces, and Catalogues.


You can also assign User Access to control who can view or edit information related to that event.
