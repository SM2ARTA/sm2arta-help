Receiving Assets at Warehouse (Inbound)


Objective


To ensure all assets received at the Main Logistics Center (Warehouse) are correctly processed through the Warehouse Management System (WMS) for operational control and then recorded in SM2ARTA for global asset tracking and event-level visibility.


Procedure

Pre-Receiving â€“ Advanced Shipment Notification (ASN)


  • Before arrival, the Material Planning Manager creates an ASN in SM2ARTA and sends it by email to Warehouse Personnel in Excel format.
  • The ASN must include:
    • SM2ARTA Transaction Number;
    • Shipment Reference and Expected Delivery Date/Time;
    • Origin / Supplier;
    • Full item list with Nomenclatures and quantities;
    • Transport details (carrier, vehicle number, seal number if available);
    • Delivery Documents (BOL, PL, TM) if available.
  • Based on the ASN, Warehouse Personnel create a Receiving Order in WMS using the SM2ARTA ASN Number for cross-reference.
  • Any shipment arriving without a receiving order must be held pending verification by the Material Planning Manager.


Example of Inbound ASN


Vehicle Arrival


Warehouse Personnel perform following activities:


  • Confirm vehicle arrival time against the planned delivery schedule.
  • Request Delivery Documents (DD) from the driver.
  • If documents are missing, locate the receiving order in WMS and print for verification.
  • Verify that the truck seal (if any) matches the seal number indicated on the DD.
  • Take photos of:
    • Truck and license plate;
    • Seal and container door (before opening if applicable).


Unloading the Vehicle


Warehouse Personnel perform following activities:


  • Ensure the unloading area is clear, safe, and suitable for the cargo type.
  • Verify Material Handling Equipment (MHE) availability (e.g., pallet jacks, forklifts).
  • Assign personnel responsible for supervising and unloading operation.
  • During unloading:
    • Handle materials carefully, using two-person verification for heavy or fragile items;
    • Inspect visible packaging for damage before moving;
    • Separate any damaged pallets;
    • Record any irregularities immediately in the DD.
  • Take photos of:
    • Pallets as they are unloaded;
    • Any damaged items or packaging.
  • If damage occurs during unloading:
    • Take detailed photos of the affected items and surrounding area;
    • Complete the Damage Report to record all circumstances (involved personnel, MHE used, packaging condition, damage type);
    • Notify the Material Planning Manager and move damaged goods to the Quarantine Zone for further inspection;
  • Once unloading is complete, move all received pallets to the receiving inspection area for further verification. Do not move items into storage until verified.


Verification Process


Warehouse Personnel perform following activities:


  • Once pallets are staged in the Receiving Inspection Area, perform full verification against the Delivery Documents or WMS Receiving Order.
  • Check and validate:
    • Nomenclature codes (SKU);
    • Quantities;
    • Units of Measurement (UoM);
    • Descriptions and item types.
  • Inspect each pallet and box again for damages or missing labels.
  • If discrepancies (missing items) or damages are identified:
    • Complete a Non-Conformance Report (NCR) detailing the findings;
    • Attach all supporting documentation and photographs.
  • Move affected items to the Quarantine Area for further inspection and resolution.
  • Once all items are verified and confirmed accurate, release them for put-away into their designated storage locations as per WMS assignment.


Receiving Confirmation


  • After verification and put-away, the Warehouse Personnel must update the WMS Receiving Order with:
    • Uploaded photos and delivery documents;
    • Entered verification notes and inspection results;
    • Any NCR or Damage Report references;
  • Once all details are complete, the receiving order can be confirmed in WMS.
  • Upon confirmation, WMS automatically sends a confirmation email.


SM2ARTA Confirmation


Material Planning Manager should:


  • Once the receiving operation is finalized and the WMS confirmation email is received, locate the corresponding transaction in SM2ARTA.
  • Enter the following information:
    • WMS Reference Number.
  • Verify and adjust the following if required
    • Date and Time of Receipt;
    • Quantities and Nomenclature Codes (SKUs) matching the WMS entry if any discrepancies;
    • Condition: Damaged / Missing.
  • Upload supporting documentation:
    • Signed Delivery Documents (DD);
    • WMS Receiving Summary Report;
    • NCR Report (if applicable);
    • Photographic evidence (arrival, unloading, inspection, damage).
  • Complete the transaction in SM2ARTA.
