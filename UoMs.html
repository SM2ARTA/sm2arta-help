UoMs

Overview


Units of Measurement (UoMs) define how material quantities are recorded, tracked, and reported in SM2ARTA.


They ensure consistency across planning, budgeting, logistics, and reporting activities.


Typical examples include:


  • Each (EA) – for individual items such as chairs or tables
  • Box (BX), Pack (PK), or Roll (RL) – for consumables or grouped supplies


Key Points:


  • Every Material must have a defined UoM before it can be created.
  • UoMs help ensure accurate budgeting, transport calculations, and stock management.
  • Administrators can manage UoMs globally to maintain consistency across all Catalogues and Events.
