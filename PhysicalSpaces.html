Physical Spaces

Overview


Physical Spaces represent the actual locations within a venue and can later be linked to Logical Spaces during detailed planning.


They may include specific rooms (e.g., Media Center, Locker Room) or broader areas (e.g., Parking Lot, Loading Bay).


Physical Spaces can be used as reference points for deliveries, installations, or collections, helping logistics teams coordinate on-site activities efficiently.


For convenience, Physical Spaces are grouped By Venues, allowing users to easily navigate and manage all areas within each site.
