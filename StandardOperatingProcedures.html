  1. Asset Tracking

Standard Operating Procedures Included in Asset Tracking


While Material Planning governs the planning stage of the project, Asset Tracking governs the operational stage and encompasses several standard operating procedures that ensure all materials are properly received, managed, distributed, and reconciled across the event footprint.


Title

Description

Primary System

Receiving Assets at Warehouse (Inbound)

Procedure for inbound deliveries — received in WMS, confirmed in SM2ARTA.

WMS → SM2ARTA

Inventory Control of Assets at Warehouse

Regular verification of stock accuracy between WMS and SM2ARTA, including location mapping, cycle counts, and adjustments.

WMS → SM2ARTA

Dispatching Assets from Warehouse (Outbound)

Picking, packing, and shipping of goods to venues; transaction mirrored in SM2ARTA.

SM2ARTA → WMS

Receiving Assets at Venue

Venue-level receiving procedure for inbound shipments from warehouse or direct suppliers; includes inspection, verification, and documentation.

SM2ARTA

Hand-over and hand-back of Assets at Venues

Delivery of assets from venue compounds to logical spaces and their return after use; supported by Room Data Sheets (RDS) and photos.

SM2ARTA

Moving Assets Between Venues

Inter-venue transfers using AITF; both sending and receiving sites must confirm.

SM2ARTA

Returning Assets to Warehouse

Final return or backhaul to WHS after event; reconciled in WMS.

SM2ARTA → WMS

Damage Reporting

Procedure for identifying and documenting damaged items.

SM2ARTA

Dissolution and Asset Closure

End-of-life or redistribution of items after event closure.

SM2ARTA / WMS
