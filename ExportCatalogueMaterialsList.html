Export Catalogue Materials List


You can export the Catalogue Materials List to Excel at any time.


This can be useful for comparing two Catalogues, sharing the list with Functional Material Planners for review or confirmation, or providing information to people who do not have access to SM2ARTA.


1. Go to Materials - Catalogues in the Navigation Panel.



2. You will see the full list of Catalogues created in the system. 



3. Click on the Catalogue from which you want to export the Materials list.



4. You will see a list of all Materials already added to that Catalogue.



5. Click the Export to Excel icon in the Action Bar.
