Sources

Overview


Sources define where a Material originates from or how it is obtained.


They help Material Planning Managers distinguish between procured, rented, retail, or service-based items, supporting clear cost tracking and dissolution planning.

Key Points


  • The Source field is optional when creating a Material and can be added or updated later once the origin or procurement method is known.
  • Defining Sources helps improve budgeting, sustainability tracking, and post-event planning (e.g., return of rental items; donation of locally procured; return of temporary imported).
  • Early classification enhances cost visibility and simplifies reporting by separating rented, owned, and service-based materials.
