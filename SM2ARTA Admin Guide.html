Material Management includes the scoping, coordination, and management of all assets and consumables required for each function to successfully deliver the tournament and its related events.

SM2ARTA is built on three fundamental principles:


Simplicity
SM2ARTA is intentionally lightweight - focusing only on the essential features needed to manage complex processes effectively. It avoids unnecessary layers often found in large enterprise systems.


Comprehensibility
The user interface is designed to be clear, intuitive, and user-friendly, enabling users to navigate and complete their tasks with ease.


Stability
By keeping the system simple and focused, SM2ARTA ensures consistent performance and reliability across all environments.


Launched in 2020, SM2ARTA continues to evolve while preserving the core principles that define its design philosophy.
